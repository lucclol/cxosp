About Us

A different kind of executive search firm

CXO Search Partners (CXOSP) is a boutique executive search firm specializing in the Life Sciences and Technology sectors. With our vision and focus on diversity, CXOSP aims to partner with those looking to drive innovation through visionary people.

Driven by the founder’s own identity as a female minority, CXOSP is committed to creating equal opportunities for exceptional candidates from all backgrounds. With deep industry knowledge and an extensive network, we identify and attract top-tier executive talent who can thrive in the rapidly evolving landscape of Life Sciences and technology.

We are not the traditional recruiting agency that works on an open role and then finds a candidate to fill it. We bring personalized solutions and professional experience that goes beyond filling positions. We start with the candidate first. We take time to know the candidate, their experience, and what type of businesses they can bring the most value to, and present them to those entities.

We represent candidates as their agent and selectively match and present them to your team. When you partner with us, you gain first access to leaders who are passively or actively looking for their next great opportunity.
